Temple

Kerala Mini Sabarimala is the only International Research Center blessed by Panthalam Valiyarajah His Holiness Ramavarma Thamburaan, and directed by Sabarimala Tantri Brhammasri Kandaruru Rajeeveruru where every devotee is permitted to perform Gee Abhisheka stepping Holy eighteen steps irrespective of Cast, Creed, Religion and Nationality. This is managed by Ayyappa Seva Ashramam (only aashramam in the world on the name of Bhagavan Ayyappsa) which is an independent shelter center, formed to study, teach, practice and propagate Sanathana Dharma and Ayyappa Cult for the Integration and Harmony of Humanity and Brotherhood.

It is located on the Holy banks of Nila River Bharathapuzha the longest river of Kerala, at Mankara village, Palakkad District, Kerala State, twenty kilometers away on Palakkad Ottapalam route on the way to Sri Bhadrakaali Temple Mankara, where the first Historical Ayyappa Kumbamela took place.

The Ayyappa Vigraha installation of this temple was done by H.H. Swami Nityananda Saraswathy International Yoga Vedanta acharya and chief of DLS Sivananda Ashram, Kerala State, on the day of Pangunni Uttaram (08-04-09). Swami Lakshmanan Nambiar of Mumbai disciple of H.H. Swami Rajeshwarananda Saraswathy (K.R Menon Guruswamy) functions as the Guruswamy of this temple. H.H. Swamy Vedananda Saraswathy chairman, Kerala Samasthana Sanyasi Sabah has done the installation of Divine Mother Rajarajeshwary in the form of Mahameru Sri Chakra on 30-01-09 as Maligapurathu Manja Maathaa Mahalakshmi. Also supported by Bhagavaan Ganesha, Bhagavan Muruga, Navagrahas, Jagad Guru Aadi Shankara Bagavatpadar (re-establisher of Sanathana Dharma), Anjaneya Swami, and Coconut Ganesha. In support there are seventeen (17) Holy Temples arround this and symbolizing the seventeen Holy Peaks on the way to Sabarimala.

Daily poojas, Homas, Vedic recitation, Study of scriptures, Research on Ayappa Cult, Purana paarayana, Daily satsanga, Training of purohits, Training of Brahmacharies and Sanyasis are the daily activities of the temple.

Organizing Mass Maha Homas and Yajnas, for Universal peace and Human integration and Brotherhood, Yoga camps, Vedic classes, classes on Shaktheya (Tantra), Astrology, and other Ancient arts are special activities for the society provided by the temple.

Services

The temple renders following regular services:
  1. Special counseling courses for family devotees on occasional cases upon request.
  2. Distribution of food and cloths for the needy.
  3. Distribution of educational books and uniforms.
  4. Conducts medical camps and blood donations camps.
  5. 24 Hours ambulance service.
  6. Co-ordination of all like minded organization for the welfare of the society.
  7. Pilgrimage trips, educational trips, and management study trips.
  8. Medical guidance for BP, Diabetics, Asthma, Fits, and Heart decease.
  9. Special Health classes for natural living style.
  10. Classes on eastern philosophy, Prasthana Trayas, and Sri Vidya upasana for upgraded seekers.
  11. Mass conference and congress on various aspects of Hindu Dharma.
  12. Spacial guidance and support for widows, family hurted and seperated people, orphans, old aged.
  13. Re-establish of decayed and damaged temples, ashrams, and other spritual centeres.
  14. Rituals and required vedic homas for individuals and mass.

Founder

H.H. Swami Prabhakarananda Saraswathy is the direct disciple of H.H. Swami Nityananda Saraswathy International Yoga Vedanta acharya and chief of DLS Sivananda Ashram under Shankaracharya Tradition (Sringagiri Peetha). His Holiness is regular visitor of Sri Lanka, Malaysia, UAE, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Mauritius, Bahrain, Nepal, U.K, Europe, China, Hong and other Gulf countries. Swamiji mainly handles Eastern philosophy, Temple Science, Yoga camps, Vedantic texts, Vedic classes, Nature cure classes, Scientific aspects of human rules and regulations, and Ayyappa Cult. In poorwasram Swamiji was a graduate engineer in electronics and communication and has served in different states, and is fluent in languages like English, Hindi, Malayalam, Sanskrit, Marathi, and Tamil.

Swamiji has visited all the four Shankaracharya Amnaya peethas, Puri (Orisa), Srigeri (Karnataka), Dwaraka (Gujarath), Badri (Uttaranjal) for penance and has received blessings from the respective aarcharyas before Sanyasa. Different honor-ships and awards came in search of Swamiji, and was rejected by His Holiness but he was Honored and Awarded as Ayyappa Bhakti Pravachana Tilakam in December 1999 at NCH Colony Kanjurmarg, by Mini Sabarimala Mumbai.

Swamiji started his Sanyasa life on 17th November 1986 with the blessings of H.H. Swami Chinmayananda Saraswathy, H.H. Swami Dayananda Saraswathy and H.H. Swami Rajeshwarananda Saraswathy at his young age. Thus Swamiji always concentrates on young generation and has initiated number of brammacharies, grahasthas, and other different order devotees. His Holiness has initiated 42 sanyasis and mathajis in Shakaracharya Tradition after his sanyasa with the blessings of His Guru, in India and abroad. Swamiji has taken the mission to wipe out unnecessary blind believes, and to train each and every seeker to worship with their own hands without losing the essence. With this goal His Holiness has conducted numerous Yagas and Homas and 206 Samooha Maha Chandika Homas in secular way for Universal Peace, Human Integration and welfare of all living beings in the society. His Holiness being a great thinker philosopher, preacher, councilor and writer, is still dynamic, with full energy of vitality, ready to serve humankind, from Ayyappa pooja and seva to Ambulance driving seva, not by sitting on the throne of the ashram, but by entering to the minds of ordinary human families and integrating them with their own simple life styles and making them practice Sanathana Dharma without hurting their existing customs and systems.

His Holiness has simple harmonious loving friendly approachable outlook and is expert in comparing the ancient traditional vedic human life values with the existing scientific life of families, and easily adopts latest simple techniques for busy working aspirants with essentials values so that they are able to follow Sanathana Dharma with their customs and systems easily in daily practical life without escaping from life.
